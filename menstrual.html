The menstrual phase begins on Day 1 of your period. Hormone levels (estrogen and progesterone) drop sharply, causing the uterine lining to shed ‚Äî your actual period.

What's happening in your body?

  • Hormone levels are at their lowest
  • You may feel tired, withdrawn, or moody
  • Cramping, bloating, and fatigue are common
  • Basal body temperature drops slightly

Self-Care Tips

Rest & Relax

Prioritize sleep and take naps when needed. Your body is working hard.

Heat Therapy

Use heating pads or warm baths to soothe cramps and relax muscles.

Gentle Movement

Try light yoga, stretching, or short walks to improve circulation.

Reflect

Journaling or meditation can help process emotions during this phase.

Foods to Support You

Iron-Rich Foods

  • Spinach & leafy greens
  • Lentils & beans
  • Beets & red meat

Magnesium Sources

  • Dark chocolate
  • Bananas
  • Nuts & seeds

Hydration Helpers

  • Herbal teas
  • Warm broths
  • Coconut water

The follicular phase begins after menstruation ends. Rising estrogen levels boost your energy and mood as your body prepares for potential ovulation.

What's happening in your body?

  • Estrogen levels steadily rise
  • Follicles mature in preparation for ovulation
  • Cervical mucus becomes thin and stretchy
  • Energy and mood typically improve

Energy Optimization

Challenge Yourself

This is the ideal time for intense workouts and trying new physical challenges.

Creative Projects

Channel your increased mental clarity into creative endeavors and problem-solving.

Socialize

Your natural charisma peaks - schedule important meetings or social events.

Learn New Skills

Your brain is primed for learning - take that class or workshop you've been considering.

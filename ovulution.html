Ovulation occurs when an egg is released from the ovary, typically around day 14. This is your most fertile window with peak estrogen and testosterone levels.

What's happening in your body?

  • LH surge triggers egg release
  • Estrogen and testosterone peak
  • Cervical mucus resembles egg whites
  • Basal body temperature rises slightly
